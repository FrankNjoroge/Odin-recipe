Sweet and Sour Meatballs

Description

This sweet and sour meatball recipe came from my mother-in-law. I got it shortly after I was married and now it is a favorite of our kids. I like to serve it over rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 cup dry bread crumbs
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 egg
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • Sweet and Sour Sauce:
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • ½ cup ketchup
  • ½ cup cider vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce

Steps

  1. Make meatballs: Mix beef, bread crumbs, onion, egg, salt, and pepper together in a large bowl until well combined.
  2. Shape mixture into 1 1/2-inch diameter balls.
  3. Cook meatballs in a large nonstick skillet over medium heat until evenly browned and cooked through.
  4. Make sauce: Mix brown sugar, water, ketchup, vinegar, soy sauce, and cornstarch together in a medium bowl until smooth.
  5. Pour sauce over meatballs. Reduce heat to low and simmer until sauce is thick, 3 to 5 minutes.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
